Scripts

This space is meant to be a repository for the interconnected absurdist comedies I began writing in my first year of college at Knox College, earning an honorable mention from the Davenport Playwriting Awards and a staged reading at the First Look Play Festival in Galesburg, Illinois the following year.

In addition, it is intended to host a drama based on a premise outlined by J.M. Barrie in his rectorial address to the University of St Andrews in the aftermath of the first world war, which I happened to stumble upon in a Washington, DC, bookstore 100 years later as the war in Ukraine was beginning.
